My Dodgeball Team

The Dodgeballers..

My dodgeball team, "The Dodgeballers..", consists of me, Miguel O'Hara, The Hulk, Aragorn, Edward Cullen, Hercules. Quite an array of characters I'd say. My goal is to absolutely crush the opposing team, and with the group I've put together here I think that will be astoundingly easy. To be frank, I'm not even entirely sure the opposing team will surive this game at all.

Miguel O'Hara

Seeing as my goal is to crush the opposing team, I think Miguel O'Hara is a critical character to add to my team. He's very strong and fast, and most importantly he's nearly always in a bad mood which means he can take his anger out on the other team.

The Hulk

This is a pretty self explanatory one, the Hulk is crazy strong and is yet another angry character who would probably love getting the opportunity to throw things at people and would definitely get super into this game.

Aragorn

The only person on my team who I think I could genuinely befriend and enjoy hanging out with. I chose Aragorn as a teammate because he is for sure a team player and is very loyal, certainly someone I would trust to have my back in any game.

Edward Cullen

Definitely the most pathetic character on this team, I think Edward is pretty awful but he's awful in such a dumb way that it makes his existence quite funny to me. He would be a great teammate for dodgeball because of his speed and strength. Plus he's basically immortal and can't get tired.

Hercules

One of the strongest characters I could think of, I knew Hercules would be an excellent addition to my team because he's pretty much unstoppable and I think someone out of a Greek myth would surely inspire some awe and intimidation in the other team.
