Ideal Type of Job for Me

I think an ideal sort of job for someone like me would be teaching in some way. I have leadership experience from being president of a club, and from that and other situations I have conflict resolution skills from interacting with a lot of people from different backgrounds who may not always see eye to eye on things and need to be approached the right way to get them to get along. I also have spent a great amount of time around my little sister who is still quite young and I've helped with taking care of her a lot and gotten experience dealing with kids from that, as well as babysitting some other kids on occasion. Being a teacher would also be ideal because it would mean that once I have kids I'd be able to be home most of the time when they're home since my work schedule would mostly line up with their school schedule so I'd get to spend more time with them that way and not have to put them in daycare constantly.
