About me:

Some of my recent favorite hobbies have been yoga and cooking, which I hadn't done very much of prior to this year but seemed like good ways to improve my life so I started getting into both this year and it's felt really nice. Another favorite hobby/interest of mine is brewing tea, and I've been doing that for many years now. If I had unlimited money one of my big goals in life would be to open a cafe, but since that's pretty hard to succeed in doing I think my goal currently is to become an art teacher at an elementary school, and if I'm lucky maybe someday I'll get the opportunity to pursue my cafe dream.
