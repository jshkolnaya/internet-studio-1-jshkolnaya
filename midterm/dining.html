Great places to eat!

Volcano Kebab

Volcano Kebab

An incredible restaurant with a plethora of food options to choose from! They've got absolutely delightful kebabs that customers rave about, they've got shawarma with various meats, they've got pizza, everyone's bound to find something they like here.

3 Frakkar (Three Coats)

3 Frakkar

A restaurant that will likely introduce you to some foods you've never had before! They've got puffin, whale, and horse steaks that you can try; certainly not something you see at most restaurants. Some people say they've had one of the best meals of their life at this place.
