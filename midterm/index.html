Iceland

Introduction

Iceland is a Nordic island country located between the Artic Ocean and the North Atlantic Ocean, between Europe and North America. Its capital is Reykjavik, with 36% of the country's population (390,000) living in it. The official language of the country is Icelandic, a North Germanic language. Iceland is the 18th largest island in the world.

Factoids

  • Iceland is on a rift between tectonic plates, which results in frequent volcanic eruptions and geysers.
  • Up until October 2025, Iceland had zero wild mosquito sightings
  • There are zero McDonald's restaurants in Iceland
  • Iceland was one of the last places on Earth to be settled by humans
