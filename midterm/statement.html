Design Statement

When I picked Iceland as my destination for this assignment, I pretty much instantly knew that whatever my animation was, it had to involve an image of the northern lights. I thought it would be really cool and fitting considering Iceland is known for being a great place to view the auroras, so that's why I thought it would be fun to have an image of them sliding across the screen sorta in the background. All the colors I chose were shades of purple and green because those are the main colors the auroras are seen in. And the html background color is meant to look like the night sky, so my animation with the northern lights image looks like it really is in the sky.

I chose sans serif fonts simply because I for some reason feel like Iceland gives off sans serif vibes and serif fonts wouldn't be as fitting for anything Iceland related in my eyes haha.
